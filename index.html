My Story

I'm driven by a simple belief: everyone deserves to feel empowered to create with technology. My journey from a CS student to a Content Designer at Autodesk taught me that learning, creating, and teaching others is where my real power lies. When I spent last summer diving deep into API Documentation, I realized something profound: mastery isn't just about knowing—it's about being able to help others discover their own potential.

What excites me most is witnessing this incredible creativity revolution we're living through. It's isn't just changing how we build—it's democratizing who gets to be a creator. Every day, I see more people than ever before building, designing, and innovating.

At Autodesk, I channel this passion into developer experiences that don't just inform—they inspire. Whether I'm crafting documentation that makes complex APIs approachable, getting feedback from real users to understand their needs, or collaborating across teams to build better tools, I'm always asking: "How can we make this more human?" Because when technology feels accessible and encouraging, people start seeing themselves not just as users, but as creators in this digital renaissance.
